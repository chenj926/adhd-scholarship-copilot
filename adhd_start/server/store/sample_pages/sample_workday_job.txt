SUMMARY
The Work Management Coordinator supports scheduling, resource alignment, and cross‑functional coordination for planned and emergent maintenance activities. You will collaborate with operations, engineering, supply chain, and EHS to ensure safe, on‑time execution.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Build and maintain short‑ and mid‑term work schedules; track progress and constraints.
- Facilitate daily/weekly planning meetings; publish action items.
- Coordinate materials, access permits, and lock‑out/tag‑out workflow with stakeholders.
- Monitor execution metrics (schedule adherence, backlog, defects) and propose improvements.
- Ensure documentation, change control, and regulatory compliance.

QUALIFICATIONS
- 3+ years in maintenance planning/scheduling, PMO, or operations coordination.
- Proficiency with work management tools (e.g., SAP PM, Maximo) and Excel.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management; able to drive decisions.
- Safety‑first mindset; knowledge of permits, isolations, and field workflows.

PREFERRED
- Experience in utilities/energy, industrial facilities, or critical infrastructure.
- Familiarity with reliability engineering concepts and formal RCAs.
