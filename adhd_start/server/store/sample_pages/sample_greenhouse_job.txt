JOB TITLE
Frontend Software Engineer (React/TypeScript)

COMPANY & ROLE PITCH
We build accessible, high‑performance web experiences for millions of users. You’ll join a small team shipping features weekly, with strong mentorship and clear growth paths.

LOCATION
Toronto, ON (Hybrid: 3 days/week in office)

EMPLOYMENT TYPE
Full‑time · Permanent

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Deliver accessible, responsive UI using React, TypeScript, and modern build tooling.
- Collaborate with design to implement components and micro‑interactions.
- Integrate REST/GraphQL endpoints; handle auth and error states.
- Write unit/integration tests; participate in code review and CI/CD.
- Partner with PM/Design on roadmaps; ship iteratively.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- 2+ years building React applications (or strong portfolio).
- Proficiency with TypeScript, modern CSS, and testing frameworks.
- Experience with accessibility (ARIA, keyboard nav) and performance basics.
- Clear communication; ability to decompose large tasks into small steps.

NICE TO HAVE
- Experience with Next.js, Vite, or Webpack.
- Familiarity with Node.js, GraphQL, or cloud (AWS/GCP).

BENEFITS (EXAMPLE)
- Competitive salary & equity; health/dental/vision.
- Learning stipend; hardware budget; parental leave.
- Flexible PTO and hybrid schedule.

HOW TO APPLY (GREENHOUSE‑STYLE)
Click **“Apply for this job”** to submit your application. You’ll typically be asked for:
- Resume (PDF or link), optional cover letter.
- Basic info (name, email, phone) and work eligibility.
- Links (GitHub, portfolio, LinkedIn) and a few short questions.
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. If there is a formal close date, it will be listed as **Application deadline: YYYY‑MM‑DD 23:59 local time**.

ACCESSIBILITY & ACCOMMODATION
We welcome applicants of all backgrounds. If you need an accommodation during the application or interview process, please let us know.

LEGAL / EEO STATEMENT (EXAMPLE)
We are an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected status.
